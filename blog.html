Is Bitcoin Sound Money?

14/07/2020

Throughout history we have used many forms of money to act as a medium of exchange between people. We transitioned to money, from the barter system, which became increasingly untenable as civilisation expanded.

Humans used precious metals like Gold and Silver for thousands of years, however, a few decades ago we transitioned to fiat which isn't backed by anything. This has led to unconstrained inflation, reducing the purchasing power that money has.
